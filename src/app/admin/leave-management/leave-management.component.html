Company Leave Management Policy

This leave management policy outlines the procedures and guidelines for requesting, approving, and managing leaves for all employees of [Company Name]. This policy aims to promote fairness, consistency, and efficiency in managing employee leaves while ensuring the smooth operation of the organization.

Types of Leave

Annual Leave : Annual leave is granted to employees for rest, relaxation, and personal pursuits. The number of annual leave days allocated varies based on the employee's length of service and position within the company.

Sick Leave : Sick leave is provided to employees who are unable to perform their duties due to illness, injury, or medical appointments. The duration and requirements for sick leave are outlined in accordance with local labor laws and company regulations.

Maternity/Paternity Leave : Maternity and paternity leave are provided to employees following the birth or adoption of a child. The duration and eligibility criteria for maternity and paternity leave adhere to legal requirements and company policies.

Unpaid Leave : Unpaid leave may be granted to employees for personal reasons or circumstances not covered by other types of leave. Unpaid leave is subject to approval by the employee's supervisor and HR department.
